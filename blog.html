FAQ
What is the best AI video tool in 2025? +
Google Veo 3 is currently the most advanced, supporting native audio and voiceover generation. Sora excels at realistic motion, Runway Gen-4 is best for professional creative control, and Kling AI is ideal for anime and portrait content. Tara AI generates optimized prompts for all of them.
Can AI videos include voiceover and audio? +
Yes — Google Veo 3 is the leading model for this. It generates synchronized voiceover, ambient sounds, music, and character dialogue natively alongside the video. Our generator has dedicated voiceover fields to help you craft the right audio prompt.
How do I control the length of my AI video? +
The best approach is to include the video length naturally in your scene description — for example, "a 15-second cinematic shot of..." or "a 30-second clip showing...". This gives the AI model precise context for pacing and content. Tara AI's generator reminds you to include this in your scene description.
Is Tara AI free to use? +

How do I write a good AI video prompt? +
A strong prompt includes: (1) a specific scene description with length, (2) visual style, (3) camera movement and angle, (4) lighting and mood, (5) voiceover or audio details. Tara AI walks you through all of these with dedicated fields for each platform.
